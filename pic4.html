Baguio City

Summer Capital

Baguio City is a charming hill station in the Philippines that often referred to as the "Summer Capital of the Philippines." Blessed with a cool climate and stunning natural landscapes to Baguio beckons travelers to escape the heat and immerse themselves in its refreshing ambiance. The city's winding roads are lined with vibrant blooms while its bustling markets offer a cornucopia of local delicacies and handicrafts. Baguio is also a haven for art and culture enthusiasts that with its numerous galleries, museums, and cultural events showcasing the rich heritage of the region. From strolling through its scenic parks to savoring the aroma of freshly brewed coffee in cozy cafes, Baguio captivates visitors with its unique blend of natural beauty and cultural vibrancy.
