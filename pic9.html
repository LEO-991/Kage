Manila City

National Museum

The National Museum of the Philippines in Manila is a symbol of the nation's rich heritage and artistic excellence. Its classical yet modern architecture houses a diverse collection spanning centuries that showcasing artifacts, artworks, and natural wonders that tell the story of the Filipino people. From ancient relics to contemporary masterpieces on the museum offers a journey through time and culture that inviting visitors to explore and appreciate the depth and beauty of Philippine history and creativity.
