Kayangan

Its is a hidden gem of crystal-clear waters nestled amidst towering limestone cliffs. Its mesmerizing beauty with vibrant turquoise waters reflecting the surrounding lush greenery and captivates all who visit. A sacred site for the indigenous Tagbanua people adventure Kayangan Lake offers serenity and natural splendor that making it a must-see destination for travelers seeking tranquility and adventure in Palawan's paradise.
