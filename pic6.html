Rice Terraces

The breathtaking Rice Terraces of the Philippine Cordilleras! Carved into the mountainsides by indigenous Ifugao communities over 2,000 years ago, these stunning terraces are a testament to human ingenuity and harmony with nature. Recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The Rice Terraces showcase the intricate farming techniques passed down through generations. Visitors are mesmerized by the cascading layers of green, reflecting the vibrant culture and deep connection to the land. Explore this living cultural landscape and marvel at the wonders of the Ifugao heritage.
