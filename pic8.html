Vigan City

Step back in time and experience the enchanting allure of Vigan City, Philippines! This UNESCO World Heritage Site is a living testament to Spanish colonial architecture, with well-preserved cobblestone streets and ancestral houses that whisper tales of centuries past. Explore the iconic Calle Crisologo that where horse-drawn carriages traverse streets lined with majestic mansions. Delight in local delicacies and artisan crafts in the bustling markets of this cultural gem. Vigan City invites you to wander its historic streets and immerse yourself in its vibrant culture and create memories that will last a lifetime.
